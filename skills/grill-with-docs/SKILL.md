name: grill-with-docs
description: "A relentless interview to sharpen a plan or design, which also creates docs (ADR's and glossary) as we go."
---

Run a `/grilling` session using the `/domain-modeling` skill.

Prose remains the source of truth for intent, terminology, trade-offs, examples, operational constraints, and why a model exists.

Use `/alloy-spec` when the clarified risk is about allowed structures: ownership, containment, cardinality, reachability, permissions, tenancy boundaries, cycles, or invalid combinations of state.

Use `/tla-spec` when the clarified risk is about allowed executions: workflows, ordering, concurrency, retries, failures, liveness, fairness, or temporal behavior.
